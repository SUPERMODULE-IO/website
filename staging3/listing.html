Blooming Dreams

Rozario created an impulsive painting that captures millions of hearts just like football lovers can let off their worries on the pitch. Since the world is engulfed in the love of football, Rozario has succeeded in bringing our favorite game to life in a perfect fusion of colors. There was a time when sports were only enjoyed by men, but the game has changed in today's world. "Blooming Dream", composed of blue, the color of openness, and red, the color of love, evokes joy and delight in everyone. This painting illustrates how football has gathered such a devoted fan base that has woven similar emotions in galleries from villages to cities. The girl in the rural shade is the loving embrace of Arab culture, the arena of the world cup. Rozario has his trademark and techniques in each of his paintings. The picture is painted with acrylic colors and a knife on canvas. His paintings stand out because of his unique mixing method and unique strokes. The blending of different colors and tones in one stroke gives the image, a pleasing spectator.

About Razi Rozario

Razi Rozario Experience is a virtue. Looking at the works of Razi Rozario puts you inside a moment to experience the laughter, the sadness, the confusion, the heartbreak, the fear, and the bliss shared in a single expression. For the past twenty-five years, He has been a full-time artist. He experiments with different flavors of color that can speak all languages. He was born on 10th April 1973, two days after the death of the famous artist Pablo Picasso, which is considered a destiny for his success as a profound artist. He says, ‘I work on my painting with immense pleasure, carefully noting all its elements. Be it a rose or a gust of wind, then I realize that I haven’t seen a rose, I don’t know how many petals are turned in and out. I keenly observe nature, and finally, I become the rose that I intended to paint and complete the picture. But a painting is complete only when the spectator receives the feel I experienced while drawing’. He is someone, who draws from his experiences, history, and a huge pile of memory behind each painting, which drags people’s attention toward his paintings. His wish was to uplift art to the masses or the common people. His paintings range from young girls, grandmothers, Jesus Christ, and Kaaba at Mecca, to sunflowers and jackfruit. He is a freelance artist, who pursued his master’s degree in Malayalam literature. He is the recipient of the Kala Prathibha award from Mahatma Gandhi University in the year 2003. He has also published a book named ‘ The Ploughed-up sky’ which is a painting poetry series and an autobiography, ‘Njaan Vashikkarana’ (Am Stubborn). He has conducted around 105 solo exhibitions nationwide and participated in the state exhibition conducted by Kerala Lalithakala academy. He has exhibited his works on college campuses, tourism fairs, shopping festival book fairs, Lulu mall, etc. His life took a U-turn when he met superstar Shri Mohanlal, he bought a painting ‘Creators of shadow’, which got selected for the state exhibition. His vision is that anything in the whole world, be it literature, music, or painting, everything belongs to every human being. Painting and other arts become unpopular they are restricted and confined to very few. His wish is to become a game-changer and provide accessibility to common people as well. He says that ‘Art is his life. There is nothing else he want to do.’
