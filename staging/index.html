Roadmap

Aug 2021

Ideation - The idea came to light during a debate on how to increase the intrensic value of digital assets.

Nov 2021

Inception - After the initial brainstorming and ressearch an offical entity was incorporated.

Jan 2022

Validation - First official prospect accepted the proposal for a cross-border solution.

Incubation - Initiated incubating at AEIC (American University of Dubai Enterpreneurship & Innocation Center).

DAO Proposal - Proposal to proceed with DAO (Decentralised Autonomous Organisation) model until the regulations are in place.
